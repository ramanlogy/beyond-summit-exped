The Experience

Pokhara is considered one of the world's finest paragliding destinations — and from the air, you'll understand why.

Launching from Sarangkot at 1,600 metres, you'll catch the famous Pokhara thermals that carry you effortlessly above the Phewa Lake valley. On a clear day, the snow-capped peaks of Annapurna, Machapuchare (Fishtail), Dhaulagiri, and Manaslu stretch across the entire northern horizon.

Our tandem flights require zero prior experience. You'll be strapped securely to one of our APPI-certified pilots — all with a minimum of 3,000 flight hours over the Pokhara valley. You simply enjoy the view.

For those seeking more, our acrobatic and extended cross-country flights offer spirals, wingovers, and thermal soaring — the same experience that makes Pokhara a training destination for professional pilots from around the world.

Questions

Before You Fly

Everything you need to know before your feet leave the ground. Don't see your question here? WhatsApp us directly and we'll answer within minutes.

Do I need any experience to paraglide?
None at all. Tandem paragliding means you fly attached to a certified pilot who controls everything. You just need to be able to run a few steps on launch and enjoy the view. We've flown guests from 5 to 75 years old.
What's the best time of year to fly?
October to May offers the clearest skies and most stable thermals. The post-monsoon (Oct–Nov) and pre-monsoon (Mar–May) windows are peak season for mountain views. We do fly year-round, though monsoon months (June–Sept) have more weather cancellations.
Is there a weight or age limit?
Maximum weight is 110 kg. Minimum age is 5 years with signed guardian consent. We also recommend guests with serious heart conditions or vertigo consult their doctor first, though most healthy adults fly without issue.
What if the weather is bad on my booked day?
Safety is non-negotiable. If conditions don't meet our standards, we postpone and rebook at no charge. We monitor forecasts daily and will contact you the evening before if we anticipate issues. Full refunds are offered if no suitable date is found during your stay.
Can I bring my own camera or phone?
Loose items must be secured — we provide a chest harness attachment point. Our GoPro footage covers all angles, and we strongly recommend using our provided system rather than a handheld phone, which can be dropped. All footage is transferred to your phone within minutes of landing.
How do I get to Sarangkot for the flight?
Transport to and from Sarangkot is included in all our packages. We pick you up from your Pokhara lakeside hotel. If you're staying elsewhere in Pokhara, let us know when booking and we'll arrange pickup from the nearest convenient point.
