Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I book?
For Everest and other 8000m peaks, we recommend booking 12–18 months ahead. Permits are limited and slots fill early. Treks can often be arranged with 4–6 weeks notice.
What experience level do I need?
It depends on the programme. EBC treks need good fitness but no technical skill. Ama Dablam needs solid mountaineering experience. Everest requires multiple 6000m+ summits. We assess every applicant individually.
Is travel insurance required?
Yes, comprehensive travel and helicopter evacuation insurance is mandatory for all expeditions. We recommend specialist high-altitude climbing policies and can suggest trusted providers.
What is included in the expedition price?
All permits, Sherpa wages, O₂ systems for high-altitude programmes, base camp meals, tents, and logistics. International flights, personal gear, and insurance are arranged by you. Full inclusions are on each programme page.
Can I do a solo private expedition?
Absolutely. Many clients prefer a fully private programme with a dedicated Sherpa team. We offer bespoke solo and small-group packages — contact us to discuss your requirements.
What is the deposit and payment schedule?
A 30% deposit confirms your place. The balance is due 60 days before departure. We accept bank transfer and major cards. A full payment schedule is included in your booking agreement.
