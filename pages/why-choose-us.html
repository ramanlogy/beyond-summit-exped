Our Story

From Two Sherpa
Climbers to Nepal's
Most Trusted Team

Beyond Summit Expeditions was born on the mountain. Founders Dawa Sherpa and Pemba Norbu — both Everest summiteers — started the company in 2015 with a single belief: that the most experienced climbers in the world should run the guiding company, not manage it from behind a desk.

From our first season on Ama Dablam to running concurrent expeditions on five peaks in Spring 2026, every decision we make traces back to one question: is this the safest, most thorough way to take our clients higher?

Based in Thamel, Kathmandu

Our office is in the heart of Nepal's trekking district. Walk in any time — our team is always here.

Government Licensed

Fully licensed by Nepal's Department of Tourism. Trekking Agency Registration No. active and verified.

IFMGA Certified Guides

All lead Sherpa guides hold international IFMGA and NMAS certification — the highest standard in mountain guiding.

40+ Countries Served

Our clients come from across the world. We speak English, French, German, Japanese, and Nepali fluently.
