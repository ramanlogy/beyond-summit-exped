Why Beyond Summit

Six Reasons Climbers
Choose Us

From Everest Base Camp to 8,000m summits — here's what sets us apart from every other operator in Nepal.

Sherpa-Founded & Led

Not a travel agency — we are climbers. Founded by Everest summiteers Dawa Sherpa and Pemba Norbu. Every decision is made by people who have been on the mountain.

Safety Is Non-Negotiable

Strict turnaround times. Wilderness medics on every expedition. Daily oxygen checks. We have walked away from summits — and we are proud of it.

Small Groups · Max 8

Your personal Sherpa guide is assigned to you before you arrive. We know your name, fitness level, and summit goals — not just your booking number.

IFMGA & UIAA Certified

All lead Sherpa guides hold international IFMGA / NMAS certification. Licensed by Nepal's Department of Tourism. ISO 21101 certified for adventure safety.

No Hidden Costs

Permits, oxygen, Sherpa wages, base camp meals — all included and disclosed upfront. What you see in our quote is exactly what you pay.

Community-Rooted

We share profits with Sherpa communities, fund Everest clean-up expeditions, and source supplies locally. The mountain gives us everything — we give back.
